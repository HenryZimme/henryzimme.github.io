# henryzimmerman.net

Source for my personal research and writing portfolio, live at [henryzimmerman.net](https://henryzimmerman.net).

I'm a student at Phillips Academy Andover working in time-domain astronomy. I'm currently preparing a VLT/ESPRESSO proposal with Dr. Bogumił Pilecki to study the merger history of a binary Cepheid in the LMC, and using information theory to optimize astronomical observing schedules. The site documents that work alongside my writing in history, philosophy, and literature.

The background renders the Yale Bright Star Catalog. Clicking a labeled sta  opens its research card or links to SIMBAD. The simulation section is built from real OGLE photometry and Pilecki+ (2022) radial velocity measurements.
