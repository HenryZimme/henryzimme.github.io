# henryzimmerman.net

Source for my personal research and writing portfolio, live at [henryzimmerman.net](https://henryzimmerman.net).

I'm a student at Phillips Academy Andover working in time-domain astronomy. I'm currently preparing a VLT/ESPRESSO proposal with Dr. Bogumił Pilecki to study the merger history of a binary Cepheid in the LMC, and coordinating a multi-continent photometric campaign on asteroid 7605 Cindygraber. The site documents that work alongside my writing in history, philosophy, and literature.

The background renders the Yale Bright Star Catalog. Clicking a labeled star opens its research card or links to SIMBAD. The simulation section is built from real OGLE photometry and Pilecki radial velocity measurements.
